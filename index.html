About

I'm a former educator at The Dalton School, where I taught middle and high school computer science. Drawn to the magic of early childhood, I'm now channeling that same love of learning into literacy, completing my Child Development Associate (CDA) credential and IMSE Pre-K Literacy Plus training to bring science-of-reading-aligned instruction to young children.

Early Literacy Classroom Program

Grounded in the science of early literacy, this program gives preschoolers the foundational skills they need to become confident readers. Each session weaves together code-focused and meaning-focused instruction through warm, intentional routines that keep young learners engaged.

Each session includes

  • Letter and sound introduction
  • Phonological awareness: identifying beginning sounds
  • Rhyme recognition
  • Syllable segmenting
  • Vocabulary building
  • Interactive read-aloud
One on one Pre-K through 2nd grade

Reading Tutoring

Every child learns differently. This one-on-one tutoring is tailored to meet your child exactly where they are, building the specific skills they need to grow as a reader with plenty of encouragement along the way.
